How this is calculated:
• BMI = (weight in lbs ÷ (height in inches)²) × 703.
• To goal = current weight − goal weight. Positive means lbs left to lose, negative means lbs left to gain.
• BMI categories shown are the standard CDC bands: <18.5 underweight, 18.5–24.9 normal, 25–29.9 overweight, 30+ obese. This is a general population metric — it doesn't account for muscle mass, so treat it as a rough signal, not a target.
